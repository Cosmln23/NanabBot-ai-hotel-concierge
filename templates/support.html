Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started with AI Hotel Suite?

Getting started is easy:

  1. Sign up for a free 7-day trial at yourdomain.com
  2. Configure your hotel information, rooms, and services
  3. Connect your WhatsApp or LINE account
  4. Print QR codes for guest rooms
  5. Start receiving and responding to guest inquiries automatically
How does the free trial work?

Your 7-day free trial includes full access to all features:

  • Unlimited AI-powered guest messages
  • WhatsApp and LINE integration
  • Task management system
  • Digital menu and QR codes
  • No credit card required to start

After the trial ends, you can upgrade to continue using AI Hotel Suite.

What messaging platforms do you support?

AI Hotel Suite currently supports:

  • WhatsApp Business API - Most popular worldwide
  • LINE Official Account - Popular in Thailand, Japan, Taiwan

More platforms coming soon!

How do I connect my PMS (Property Management System)?

Go to Settings > Integrations in your dashboard. We support:

  • Cloudbeds (Coming Soon) - Click "Connect" and authorize via OAuth
  • Other PMS - Contact support for custom integrations

PMS integration enables automatic guest recognition and personalized responses.

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel anytime:

  1. Go to Settings > Subscription
  2. Click "Manage Subscription"
  3. Select "Cancel Subscription"

You'll retain access until the end of your billing period. No future charges will be made.

What languages does the AI chatbot support?

AI Hotel Suite's AI automatically detects and responds in the guest's language. We support 40+ languages including:

  • English, Thai, Chinese, Japanese, Korean
  • Spanish, French, German, Italian, Portuguese
  • Russian, Arabic, Hindi, and many more

Technical Support

Common Issues

  • Messages not being received: Check your WhatsApp/LINE integration status in Settings
  • AI responses not accurate: Update your hotel information and AI personality settings
  • QR codes not working: Regenerate QR codes from the QR Code section

System Requirements

  • Modern web browser (Chrome, Safari, Firefox, Edge)
  • WhatsApp Business Account or LINE Official Account
  • Stable internet connection
